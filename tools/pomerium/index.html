Pomerium

EU Cloud & Hosting

Identity-aware reverse proxy, successor to now obsolete oauth_proxy. It inserts an OAuth step before proxying your request to the backend, so that you can safely expose your self-hosted websites to public Internet. ([Source Code](https://github.com/pomerium/pomerium))
