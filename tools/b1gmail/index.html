b1gMail

EU Email & Communication

Complete email solution that runs on any webspace with PHP and MariaDB. It supports POP3 catchall mailboxes and can also integrate with Postfix or b1gMailServer if you're running your own server. ([Source Code](https://codeberg.org/b1gMail/b1gMail), [Clients](https://www.b1gmail.eu/en/start/addon-b1gmailserver/))
